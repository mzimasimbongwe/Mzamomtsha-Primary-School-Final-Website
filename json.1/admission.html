ADMSSION 2024


Online applications 2023 for 2024 enrollment.

The Western Cape education department opened online enrolments for 13 March 2023 until the 14th of April 2023.
We urge all parents and guardians of especially our Grade R and 8 learners, for placement timeously, please see website below.
https://admissions.westerncape.gov.za/admissions/login!

Parents or Guardians MUST upload the following supporting documents:
  • Last attended school report of the learner.
  • Certified ID/passport copy of the learner.
  • Immunisation card (Road to Health Chart).


    • PLEASE CONTACT THE SCHOOL IF YOU NEED ANY ASSISTANCE WITH THE ONLINE APPLICATION
