  • About Us

    Mzamomtsha Primary School is located between Khayelistha Township and Umfuleni Township in the Western Cape Province of the Republic of South Africa. The school’s headmaster, Mr. Simphiwe Khuze, joined in 2007. The school is in a semi-township called Drift Sands..

    Vision

    To develop our learners historically, to communicate effectively in English and become self-disciplined, responsible adults, with a sound value system and to have the co-operation and full involvement of parents so that the school becomes a leader in the community..

    Mission

    For us to reach our vision for Mzamomtsha primary school, we endeavor to: · Promote freedom of belief, speech and association and embrace diverse cultures all lead by a high standard of norms and values. · Empower all learners to reach their full potentials · Establish effective communication channels between all stakeholders · Develop a well-defined and transparent management plan.
